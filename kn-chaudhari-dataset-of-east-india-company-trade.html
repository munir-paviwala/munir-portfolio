About this Dataset

This dataset provides a cleaned and digital version of the trade data originally compiled by K. N. Chaudhuri in his work The Trading World of Asia and the English East India Company (1978).

While Chaudhuri's work is a cornerstone of economic history, the data has often been difficult for modern researchers to utilize directly in digital formats. I have cleaned, structured, and updated the data to make it open and accessible for anyone wishing to conduct further research or quantitative analysis.

Scope & Coverage

  • Time Period: 1660 to 1760
  • Source: Chaudhari, K. N. (1978). The Trading World of Asia and the English East India Company: 1660-1760. Cambridge University Press.
  • Format: Structured rows and columns suitable for import into R, Python, or Tableau.
